# Code of Conduct

This project exists to make AI systems more respectful of the people who use them. We expect the same standard inside the community that we're building into the code.

## Our standard

- Be direct and honest, and be kind about it.
- Assume good faith; disagree on ideas, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks — in issues, PRs, discussions, or anywhere else this project shows up.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, or contributions that don't align with this standard, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for repeated or serious violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a confidential issue or contact the maintainers directly through the repository.
